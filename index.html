How It Works?

Create and launch your own meme token on Shibarium with ease! Here’s how our Meme Token Launchpad works:

  • Connect Wallet: Use MetaMask to connect to the Shibarium network. Ensure you have BONE for gas fees.
  • Create Token: Enter a name and symbol to deploy your meme token. A fixed supply is minted, with a portion allocated to the owner and the rest for fair minting.
  • Fair Minting: Users can mint tokens by sending BONE (1 BONE = 50,000 tokens, up to 490M tokens total). Track progress in "My Created Tokens".
  • Launch: Once 490M tokens are minted, the owner can start the launch, adding liquidity to WoofSwap and locking LP tokens.
  • Query Tokens: Check your tokens or any token’s status (minted amount, progress, etc.) using its address.
  • Secure & Transparent: Built on Shibarium with non-reentrant safeguards and clear tokenomics for fair launches.
