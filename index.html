How It Works?

Launch your meme token on Shibarium with our Meme Token Launchpad! It’s fast, fair, and powered by the BONE community. Here’s the process:

  • Connect Your Wallet: Use MetaMask to connect to Shibarium (chain ID: 109). Ensure you have enough BONE for gas fees.
  • Create Your Token: Enter a name (e.g., “ShibaRocket”) and symbol (e.g., “SHK”). Total supply is 1 billion tokens:
    • 2% (20M tokens) minted to creator (non-refundable).
    • 98% (980M tokens) for community minting and liquidity.
  • Fair Minting: Community mints tokens with BONE:
    • 1 BONE = 50,000 tokens (minimum 1 BONE).
    • Up to 490M tokens can be minted.
    • Max 1,000 BONE per wallet.
    • Users can refund tokens before launch.
  • Launch Token: At 490M tokens minted, creator launches:
    • Remaining 490M tokens paired with collected BONE for WoofSwap liquidity.
    • LP tokens locked permanently.
  • Query Token: Check token details (name, symbol, status, balance) by entering its address.
  • Fair & Secure: Built on Shibarium for low fees, with transparent tokenomics and secure mint/refund functions.
