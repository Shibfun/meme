How It Works?

Launch your meme token on Shibarium with our Meme Token Launchpad! It’s fast, fair, and powered by the BONE community. Here’s the WOOF-tastic process with all the juicy details:

  • Connect Your Wallet: Fire up MetaMask and connect to the Shibarium network (chain ID: 109). Make sure your wallet has enough BONE to cover gas fees for creating and managing your token. No BONE? Grab some on WoofSwap, pup!
  • Create Your Token: Enter a catchy name (e.g., “ShibaRocket”) and symbol (e.g., “SHK”) to deploy your meme token. Here’s how the 1 billion total supply (1,000,000,000 tokens, 18 decimals) is allocated at creation:
    • 2% (20,000,000 tokens) is minted directly to you, the creator, as a reward for your pawsome idea.
    • 98% (980,000,000 tokens) is minted to the token contract, reserved for fair minting by the community and liquidity provision after launch.
  • Fair Minting Phase: Invite the community to mint your token by sending BONE to the contract. The rules are crystal clear:
    • 1 BONE = 50,000 tokens (minimum mint: 1 BONE, multiples of 1 BONE only).
    • Up to 490,000,000 tokens (49% of total supply) can be minted by users during this phase.
    • Each wallet can contribute up to 1,000 BONE (equivalent to 50,000,000 tokens) to keep things fair.
    • Track the minting progress in real-time under “My Created Tokens” with a shiny progress bar showing how close you are to the 490M goal!
    • Before launch, users can request a refund by returning their tokens, receiving 1 BONE per 50,000 tokens sent back.
  • Launch Your Token: Once 490,000,000 tokens are minted, you (the owner) can trigger the launch. Here’s what happens:
    • The remaining 490,000,000 tokens (49% of total supply) held in the contract are paired with all collected BONE (up to 9,800 BONE if fully minted).
    • This creates a liquidity pool on WoofSwap.
    • The resulting LP tokens are sent to a secure locker address to ensure trust and transparency - Permanent lock.
    • Trading begins, and your token is live on Shibarium!
  • Query Token Status: Curious about your token or someone else’s? Enter the token address in “Query Token Info” to see:
    • Name, symbol, and launch status (minting or launched).
    • Minted amount vs. the 490M mint limit, with a progress bar.
    • Perfect for tracking your project or scouting other WOOF-worthy tokens!
  • Fair, Secure, and Transparent: Our Launchpad is built on Shibarium for low fees and high speed. Key features:
    • Contract checks ensure only real users (not bots) can mint.
    • Clear tokenomics: 2% to creator, 49% to fair minting, 49% to liquidity.
    • Ownership can be renounced or transferred for added flexibility.
